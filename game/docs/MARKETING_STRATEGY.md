# 8-Bit RPG Game Marketing Strategy

## Executive Summary

This comprehensive marketing strategy outlines our approach to building brand awareness, player acquisition, and community engagement for our 8-bit RPG game built on Godot 4.2. The strategy aligns with our indie game positioning while maximizing organic growth and community-driven development.

---

## 1. Brand Identity Development

### Core Brand Elements

**Name:** 8-bit RPG (working title)
**Tagline:** "Adventure Awaits in Every Pixel"
**Genre:** Retro-style 8-bit RPG Adventure
**Platform:** PC (Godot 4.2, GL Compatibility)

### Brand Personality
- **Nostalgic** - Celebrates classic RPGs while feeling fresh
- **Authentic** - Indie-developed, community-focused
- **Accessible** - Easy to pick up, depth to master
- **Charming** - Pixel art aesthetic with heart

### Visual Identity Guidelines

| Element | Specifications |
|---------|---------------|
| Resolution | 320x180 (upscaled to 960x540) |
| Color Palette | Limited retro palette (16-32 colors) |
| Font Style | Pixel/retro bitmap fonts |
| Logo Style | Pixel art with classic RPG motifs |
| Icon | res://assets/sprites/icon.png |

### Brand Voice
- Warm and welcoming
- Developer diary transparency
- Community-first communication
- Playful yet informative

## 4. Content Marketing Approach

### Developer Diaries (Monthly)

**Format:** Long-form blog post + YouTube video (5-10 minutes)
**Distribution:** itch.io devlog, YouTube, Twitter thread, Reddit

**Topics:**
1. The Journey Begins - Why we're making an 8-bit RPG
2. Art of Pixels - Our pixel art process and philosophy
3. Combat Chronicles - Designing the battle system
4. World Building - Creating memorable NPCs and dialogue
5. Technical Tales - Godot 4.2 development insights
6. Player Stories - Community spotlights and feedback implementation
7. Music & Sound - Retro audio design
8. Launch Day Approaches - Final polish and release prep

### Content Calendar Framework

| Week | Dev Diary | YouTube | Twitter/X | TikTok | Reddit |
|------|-----------|---------|-----------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Deep dive post | Dev diary video | Daily updates | 1-2 shorts | Weekly thread |
| 2 | Progress update | Gameplay clip | Daily updates | 1-2 shorts | Engagement |
| 3 | Technical article | Tutorial | Daily updates | 1-2 shorts | AMA thread |
| 4 | Community spotlight | Highlights | Daily updates | 1-2 shorts | Update post |

### Content Types

**Twitter/X Content Mix:**
- 30% Work-in-progress screenshots/gifs
- 25% Polished reveals and announcements
- 20% Community engagement and retweets
- 15% Behind-the-scenes insights
- 10% Fun/pixel art appreciation

**YouTube Content Types:**
- Monthly dev diaries (main content)
- Gameplay walkthrough videos
- Technical tutorials for Godot developers
- Community Q&A sessions
- Launch trailer and update trailers

## 5. Influencer Partnerships

### Tiered Influencer Strategy

**Tier 1: Macro Influencers (100K+ followers)**
- Target: 3-5 streamers/YouTubers in indie game/NES/speedrunning space
- Approach: Exclusive early access, custom content
- Deliverables: Full playthrough videos, reviews
- Timeline: Pre-launch 2 weeks for embargo release

**Tier 2: Mid-Tier Influencers (10K-100K followers)**
- Target: 10-20 indie game focused creators
- Approach: Demo builds, press kits, social media cross-promotion
- Deliverables: Preview videos, social posts, wishlist drives

**Tier 3: Micro Influencers (1K-10K followers)**
- Target: 50+ indie game enthusiasts, pixel art fans
- Approach: Community keys, retweet support
- Deliverables: Let's plays, reviews, word-of-mouth

### Influencer Outreach Templates

**Initial Contact:**
```
Subject: Early Access: 8-bit RPG - Exclusive Preview Opportunity

Hi [Name],

I'm [Developer], creating an 8-bit RPG that channels the spirit of classic 
NES adventures while introducing modern quality-of-life features.

Your content on [specific video/topic] perfectly aligns with our target audience, 
and I'd love to offer you an exclusive early access build.

Key highlights:
- Authentic 8-bit pixel art (320x180 resolution)
- Classic turn-based combat with modern polish
- Godot 4.2 engine (GL Compatibility)
- Story-driven narrative with memorable characters

Would you be interested in a preview build? Happy to provide additional 
assets, interviews, or customize content for your channel.

Best,
[Developer Name]
```

## 8. Community Engagement Strategies

### Engagement Programs

**Beta Testing Program:**
- Closed beta access for Discord superfans
- Structured feedback forms
- Test branch name: "beta-testing"
- Recognition in credits

**Community Content Program:**
- Fan art competitions (monthly)
- Speedrun leaderboards
- Story/character fan fiction spotlight
- Community-designed NPC contest

**Regular Events:**
| Event | Frequency | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Dev Q&A | Monthly | Transparency, connection |
| Speedrun challenge | Quarterly | Competitive engagement |
| Fan art showcase | Monthly | Community celebration |
| Playalong sessions | Bi-weekly | Community bonding |
| AMA (Ask Me Anything) | Monthly on Reddit | Broad reach |

### Moderation & Guidelines

**Community Code of Conduct:**
1. Be respectful to all community members
2. Constructive criticism only
3. No spoilers outside marked channels
4. No piracy or unauthorized distribution discussion
5. Support indie development spirit

**Moderation Team:**
- 1 Head Moderator (community manager)
- 3-5 Active Moderators (trusted community members)
- Auto-moderation bots for spam filters

### Feedback Loop Integration

```
Player Feedback → Triage (Bug/Feature/General)
                  ↓                    ↓
           Bug Tracker              Feature Board
                  ↓                    ↓
              Weekly Review ← ← ← ← ← ← Community Input
                  ↓
            Development Sprint
                  ↓
           Update + Announcement
                  ↓
            Community Thanks
```
